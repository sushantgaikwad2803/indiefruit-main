About Indiefruit

Indiefruit is a global import-export company of fresh fruit & vegetables, of Indian origin, the company has experience of more that a decade in the fresh fruit & vegetable business.

Indiefruit has a wide portfolio of over 14 produce items but focuses on grapes, pomegranates, bananas, onions and mangos. The company works with more than 1000 farmers, 50+ distributors & supply its quality fruits & vegetables in Middle East & Southeast Asia.
